Olivier Ngizwenimana

Public Relations and Resource Mobilization Specialist

Mr. Olivier Ngizwenimana is an expert in project development, renowned for his work against human trafficking, especially among youth. Holding a Master's Degree in Philosophy, he combines analytical thinking with problem-solving skills. His experience in leadership and public management, alongside his studies and writings on projects and business, has given him a comprehensive understanding of human trafficking complexities. As a consultant to institutions like the Basilica Saint Anne in Jerusalem and the Formation Center in Beira, Mozambique, Olivier has provided crucial insights on project development, driving positive change. In his career, Olivier has been dedicated to empowering and supporting youth. He has served as Managing Director at organizations such as Kigali Monitor, Hitamo High Technology, Kigali Art and High Definition Center, and the Golden Foundation Center (GFC). As a youth leader in the Huguka Dukore Akazi Kanoze project funded by USAID, he has significantly impacted young people's lives. Olivier's exceptional leadership, strategic thinking, and passion for impactful change have earned him a reputation as an influential figure in human trafficking prevention. His expertise and dedication uniquely position him to make a substantial difference in combating human trafficking among the youth.
